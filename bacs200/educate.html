TED Talks

Inside the mind of a master procrastinator - Tim Urban

  • Tim talks about his procrastinating in college and why he did it.
  • He explains what is different from a non procrastinators brain and a procrastinators brain.
  • A procrastinator has a rational decision mker but also has a instant gratification monkey.
  • The rational decision maker is good, but the monkey is what is holding you back from doing your work.

A simple way to break a bad habit - Judson Brewer

  • Judson starts off by explaining positive reinforcement and negative reinforcement as one of the learning processes.
  • He explains that by changing the cognitive thinking process you can break a habit by just being curious about what you are doing when you're doing that habit.
  • Mindfulness and paying attention what we are doing to ourself will allow us to be aware of our bad habits and stop them.

The next outbreak? We're not ready - Bill Gates

  • We have spent so much money on nuclear deterrents but not enough in a system to stop an epidemic.
  • Bill talks about Ebola and how we weren't prepared for a mass epidemic like that.
  • "Theres no need to panic but we need to get going."
